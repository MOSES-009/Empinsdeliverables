Visa Solutions for UK Business Visits

The UK is one of the most vibrant economies in the world. Its role in the global economy makes it one of the top destinations for business persons worldwide. To visit the UK for business, you must apply for up to 3 months before you travel. Empins can guide you through the UK business visa process with our expert services.

UK Business Visa Details

The UK business visa is a one-time or longer-term visa that allows holders to stay in the UK for up to 6 months. You must apply for this visa if you are visiting the UK for reasons such as:
You are attending a conference, meeting or training
You are taking part in sports activity
You are looking for funding to start, are over,join or run a business

Other business activities

Employees of a corporation based in another country can also engage in the following corporate or intra-corporate activities.
Share skills and knowledge on a specific internal project with UK employees of the same corporate group, provided no work is done directly with clients, advice, consult, troubleshooting or provide training.
As an internal auditor for a UK branch of the same group of companies as your employer overseas, conducts regulatory or financial audits.
Receive training in work methods and procedures essential for the visitor's employment abroad and are unavailable in their home country from a UK-based company or organization.

Eligibility requirements

You must be at least 18 years old.
You must be able to support yourself for the duration of your visit financially.
You must meet the authorities' basic health requirements.
You must have a legal invitation from a legitimate company in the country with whom you are/will conduct business.
