ABOUT EMPINS TRAVEL AGENCY
Over 4 years of experience in Travel Consultancy

Empins Travel Agency was formed in the year 2018 as start up of only five employees. The company is based at View Park towers 13th floor along Utalii Street Nairobi, Kenya. Empins Travel Agency has had a gradual increase from serving 10 clients per week to serving over 100 clients per week currently. We started with offering to one country and have levelled up to more than ten countries so far.
